Game Rules

Objective

Brain games make your brain active always.

How To Play

1. you have to select players count, cards type, grid size.

2. If only one player play, he fliped all the card with free time play.

3. In turn, each player has 15 seconds only to match many cards as he can.

4. If he matches two cards wrong. he wait until next turn to able to play again.

5. During playing, you see the results of correct/ wrong matched.

6. The game ends when all cards all flipped.
